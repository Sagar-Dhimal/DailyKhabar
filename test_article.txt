Barcelona are celebrating winning La Liga for the second season in a row, after victory over Real Madrid confirmed their successful title defence.

Hansi Flick’s side defeated their Clasico rivals 2-0 at the Camp Nou on Sunday, further stretching their lead at the top of the table to 14 points with just three games to play, making it impossible to catch them.

In Flick’s debut campaign last term, Barca won the domestic treble of La Liga, Copa del Rey and Supercopa de Espana titles. They also won La Liga in 2022-23, making this three league titles in four seasons.

Marcus Rashford gave Hansi Flick's side an early lead with a beautiful free kick into the top corner, before Ferran Torres turned in to make it two.

Barcelona only needed a point to confirm their status as back-to-back La Liga champions, winning the title in the derby match against their greatest rivals for the first time.
